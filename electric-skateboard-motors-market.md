<p><p>The electric skateboard motors market is primarily categorized into four types: wheel hub motors, belt-driven motors, direct drive motors, and others. Wheel hub motors are integrated into the wheel for efficient power delivery and compact design. Belt-driven motors use pulleys and belts for improved torque and speed control. Other types include gear-driven motors and unique designs, catering to varying performance needs and rider preferences.</p></p>
