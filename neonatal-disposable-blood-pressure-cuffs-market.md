<p><p>The neonatal disposable blood pressure cuffs market is divided into two main types: one tube and two tube cuffs. One tube neonatal disposable blood pressure cuffs feature a single channel for air inflation and deflation, making them simpler and often more compact. In contrast, two tube neonatal disposable blood pressure cuffs have separate tubes for inflation and deflation, providing more precise control over pressure measurements. Both types are designed to ensure accurate blood pressure monitoring in delicate neonatal patients, enhancing safety and efficiency in healthcare settings.</p></p>
