<p><p>Real-Time Location System (RTLS) solutions utilize various technologies for tracking assets and personnel in real-time. Ultra-Wideband (UWB) offers precise location accuracy, making it ideal for high-density environments. Wi-Fi RTLS leverages existing networks for ease of integration, while RFID provides cost-effective asset tracking using radio waves. ZigBee is energy-efficient for low-power applications, suitable for monitoring. Infrared works well in line-of-sight tracking with minimal interference. Other technologies may include Bluetooth and GPS, catering to different operational needs and environments.</p></p>
