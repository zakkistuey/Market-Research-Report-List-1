<p><p>The porous titanium filter market is categorized into two main types: tube type and plate type. In contrast, plate type filters feature flat plates with porous titanium structures, providing a larger surface area for filtration. This design enhances filtration efficiency and is commonly used in industries requiring high precision and performance, such as aerospace and medical applications.</p></p>
