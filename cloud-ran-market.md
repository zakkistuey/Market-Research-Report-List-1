<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cloud-RAN Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BBU (Baseband Units)</li><li>RRU (Remote Radio Units)</li><li>Optical Transport Network</li><li>Servers</li><li>Processors</li><li>Measurement Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cloud-RAN market consists of several key components: </p><p>1. **BBUs (Baseband Units)** manage digital signal processing and are critical for controlling multiple RRUs.</p><p>2. **RRUs (Remote Radio Units)** handle radio transmission and reception, connecting to BBUs via high-speed links.</p><p>3. **Optical Transport Networks** facilitate fast, reliable communication between BBUs and RRUs using fiber optics.</p><p>4. **Servers** support data processing and storage in a centralized manner.</p><p>5. **Processors** optimize performance and efficiency of cloud applications.</p><p>6. **Measurement Devices** monitor network performance and ensure quality of service.</p></p>
