<p><p>Conductive polymer electrolytic capacitors are designed for high-performance applications, available mainly in two market types: single-ended and surface mount. Single-ended capacitors typically feature leads for through-hole mounting, making them suitable for traditional circuit designs. In contrast, surface mount capacitors offer a compact alternative, allowing for efficient assembly on printed circuit boards (PCBs) and benefiting modern, space-constrained applications. Both types leverage conductive polymers to enhance performance characteristics, including improved stability, lower equivalent series resistance (ESR), and higher ripple current capabilities.</p></p>
