<p><p>The inflatable packaging equipment market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on bubble sheet output and air pillow output. Bubble sheet output involves creating protective, air-filled bubbles that cushion fragile items during shipping and handling. This type offers excellent shock absorption and surface protection. On the other hand, air pillow output generates lightweight inflatable pillows that fill empty spaces in packaging, preventing movement and damage to products. Both types enhance product safety while optimizing shipping efficiency and reducing material waste.</p></p>
