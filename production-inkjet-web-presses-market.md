<p><strong>The Production Inkjet Web Presses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drop-on-demand</li><li>Continuous Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Production inkjet web presses are categorized into two main types: drop-on-demand and continuous feed. Drop-on-demand technology ejects ink only when needed, enabling high resolution and precise imaging, ideal for short runs and variable printing, such as personalized marketing materials. In contrast, continuous feed presses eject ink continuously onto a substrate, allowing for high-speed, large-volume printing, making them suitable for long runs, such as magazines and newspapers. Both technologies cater to different business needs in the printing industry.</p></p>
