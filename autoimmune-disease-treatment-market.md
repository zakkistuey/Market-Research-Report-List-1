<p><p>The autoimmune disease treatment market encompasses various drug types aimed at managing symptoms and controlling immune responses. Immunosuppressants inhibit immune activity to prevent tissue damage, while anti-inflammatory drugs reduce inflammation associated with autoimmune disorders. Corticosteroids provide rapid relief by suppressing inflammation and immune responses. Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) alleviate pain and inflammation without affecting the immune system significantly. Biologics are advanced therapies targeting specific pathways in the immune response, offering targeted treatment options for various autoimmune conditions.</p></p>
