<p><p>Veterinary cardiac monitoring devices primarily include Holter monitors, event recorders, and other specialized equipment. Holter monitors provide continuous, 24-hour ECG recordings to detect arrhythmias and assess heart function. Event recorders, on the other hand, are portable devices that record heart activity during specific episodes, allowing for targeted analysis of irregularities. Other devices may encompass echocardiograms and telemetric monitors, which support diagnosing and managing various cardiac conditions in animals. Collectively, these tools enhance veterinary cardiology practices.</p></p>
