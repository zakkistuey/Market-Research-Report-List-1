<p><p>The Smart Worker Market encompasses various connectivity types to enhance workforce efficiency. BLE/Bluetooth facilitates short-range communication, ideal for devices that require low power and quick data transfer, such as wearables. LPWAN (Low Power Wide Area Network) supports long-range connectivity for IoT devices, allowing real-time monitoring over vast areas with low energy consumption. WFAN (Wireless Field Area Network) focuses on wider coverage for industrial applications, combining the benefits of both BLE and LPWAN, ensuring robust communication in diverse work environments.</p></p>
