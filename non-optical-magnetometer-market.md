<p><strong>The Non Optical Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Magnetometer</li><li>Relative Magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-optical magnetometer market is divided into absolute and relative magnetometers. Absolute magnetometers provide precise measurement of magnetic field strength without the need for calibration, offering direct readings in microteslas or nanoteslas. In contrast, relative magnetometers measure changes in magnetic fields relative to a baseline, making them ideal for applications that monitor magnetic field variations. Both types serve critical functions in geophysics, mineral exploration, and environmental monitoring, catering to diverse industry needs with their unique measurement capabilities.</p></p>
