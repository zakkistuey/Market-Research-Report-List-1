<p><p>The adhesives and tapes market is segmented into various types, including pressure-sensitive adhesives, hot-melt adhesives, light-cured adhesives, and others. Pressure-sensitive adhesives bond upon contact and do not require heat or moisture. Hot-melt adhesives, applied in a molten state, solidify upon cooling. Light-cured adhesives utilize ultraviolet light for rapid curing, providing strong bonds quickly. Other types may include solvent-based and water-based adhesives, offering diverse solutions for industries ranging from automotive to construction, driven by specific application needs.</p></p>
