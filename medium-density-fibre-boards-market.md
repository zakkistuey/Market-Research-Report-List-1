<p><p>Medium Density Fibre Boards (MDF) are categorized into two main types based on their application and properties. Type I MDF, also known as standard MDF, is suitable for indoor use, offering good stability and versatility in furniture and cabinetry. Type II MDF, or moisture-resistant MDF, is designed for environments with higher humidity, making it ideal for kitchens and bathrooms. Both types are popular in the woodworking industry, providing options to meet varying durability and moisture resistance needs.</p></p>
