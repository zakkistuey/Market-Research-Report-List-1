<p><p>The water-based PVDF (polyvinylidene fluoride) coating market is divided into two primary types: powder and granules. Powder coatings are typically applied using electrostatic spray techniques, offering superior adhesion and durability. They provide an even finish and are easy to handle. Granules, on the other hand, are often used in production processes requiring specific melting and application techniques, providing versatility in various industrial applications. Both types cater to eco-friendly demands due to their low volatile organic compound emissions.</p></p>
