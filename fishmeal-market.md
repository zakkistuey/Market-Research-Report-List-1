<p><strong>The Fishmeal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam dried(SD)</li><li>Flame dried(FD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fishmeal market includes two primary types: steam dried (SD) and flame dried (FD) fishmeal. Steam dried fishmeal is produced using steam heat, retaining higher nutritional value and amino acids, making it ideal for high-quality animal feed and aquaculture. In contrast, flame dried fishmeal involves direct contact with flame, resulting in a slightly lower nutritional profile but often at a reduced cost. Both types cater to different segments of the pet food and livestock industries, depending on specific dietary requirements.</p></p>
