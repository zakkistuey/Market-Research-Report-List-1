<p><p>Netbanking market types can be categorized into three main types: Informational, Communicative, and Transactional. The Informational type provides users with essential banking details, such as account balances and interest rates, without interactive features. The Communicative type offers messaging and support services, allowing customers to interact with bank representatives for inquiries and assistance. Lastly, the Transactional type enables customers to perform financial activities, such as fund transfers and bill payments, directly through the online banking platform, facilitating convenient banking experiences.</p></p>
