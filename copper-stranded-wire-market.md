<p><p>The copper stranded wire market is primarily categorized into two types: solid copper stranded wire and soft copper stranded wire. Solid copper stranded wire consists of multiple copper strands twisted together, providing enhanced flexibility and conductivity, making it suitable for various electrical applications. Soft copper stranded wire, on the other hand, features softer metal strands, offering higher malleability and better performance in applications requiring bending or twisting. Both types are widely used in electrical, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
