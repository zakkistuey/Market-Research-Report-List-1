<p><p>The silver-based conductive ink market includes three main types: silver nanoparticles, silver flakes, and silver nanowires. Silver nanoparticles are tiny particles that provide excellent conductivity and are often used in high-resolution printing applications. Silver flakes are larger and offer high conductivity with good mechanical properties, making them suitable for thicker inks. Silver nanowires combine high conductivity with flexibility, ideal for applications like transparent conductive films. Each type caters to specific requirements in electronics, sensors, and other advanced applications.</p></p>
