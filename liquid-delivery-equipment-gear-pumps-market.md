<p><strong>The Liquid Delivery Equipment Gear Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Internal Gear Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Delivery Equipment Gear Pumps market primarily comprises two types: external gear pumps and internal gear pumps. External gear pumps utilize two interlocking gears to create flow, typically suited for higher-viscosity liquids and providing consistent flow rates. Internal gear pumps, on the other hand, feature a gear inside another gear, allowing for greater efficiency and versatility in handling a wider range of fluids, including low-viscosity liquids. Both types are essential in various industrial applications for fluid transfer and delivery.</p></p>
