<p><p>The herbal supplement market is categorized into two main types: mono-herb and multi-herb. Mono-herb supplements focus on a single plant or herb, leveraging its unique properties for targeted health benefits. In contrast, multi-herb supplements combine various herbs to provide a synergistic effect, enhancing overall efficacy and addressing multiple health concerns simultaneously. The choice between these types depends on consumer preferences, specific health goals, and the desired complexity of the herbal formulation.</p></p>
