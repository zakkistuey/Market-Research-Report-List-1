<p><strong>The Biotherapeutics Cell Line Development Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transfection & Selection</li><li>Single Cell Cloning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biotherapeutics cell line development market comprises two primary types: transfection and selection, and single cell cloning. Transfection and selection involve introducing foreign DNA into host cells, followed by selecting those expressing the desired protein. This method is crucial for producing recombinant proteins and antibodies. Single cell cloning, on the other hand, focuses on isolating individual cells to create homogeneous cell populations. Both approaches are integral for developing stable cell lines used in biopharmaceutical production, ensuring consistency and efficacy in therapeutics.</p></p>
