<p><p>The VR Head Mounted Display Device market is segmented into two primary types: All-In-One Machines and Split Types. All-In-One Machines integrate all necessary components, such as processing power and display, into a single headset, offering portability and ease of use. In contrast, Split Type devices consist of separate components, typically a headset for visual display and an external computing unit, allowing for enhanced performance and upgrade flexibility. Both types cater to different user preferences and application requirements in virtual reality experiences.</p></p>
