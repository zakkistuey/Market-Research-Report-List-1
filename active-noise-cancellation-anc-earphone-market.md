<p><strong>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) Earphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Feedforward Active Noise Canceling</li><li>Feedback ANC</li><li>Hybrid ANC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) earphone market features three primary types: Feedforward ANC uses external microphones to pick up ambient noise, canceling it before reaching the ear. Feedback ANC utilizes internal microphones to monitor sound within the ear, adjusting the cancellation in real time. Hybrid ANC combines both methods for enhanced performance, leveraging external and internal microphones. Each type targets different noise environments, catering to consumer preferences for effective sound isolation in various settings.</p></p>
