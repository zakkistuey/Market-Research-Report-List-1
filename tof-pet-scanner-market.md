<p><p>The TOF-PET scanner market comprises three main types: PET scanners, PET-CT scanners, and PET-MRI scanners. </p><p>PET scanners use positron emission tomography to visualize metabolic processes in the body. PET-CT scanners combine PET imaging with computed tomography, providing detailed anatomical and functional information. PET-MRI scanners integrate PET with magnetic resonance imaging, offering enhanced soft tissue contrast and superior spatial resolution. Each type serves unique clinical needs, impacting diagnosis, treatment planning, and patient management across diverse medical fields.</p></p>
