<p><p>The Ram Pump market encompasses several types of pumps, each serving distinct applications. Lift pumps are designed to elevate water from a lower to a higher elevation, ideal for irrigation. Force pumps generate high pressure to move fluids through pipelines, commonly used in firefighting and industrial processes. Axial piston pumps use rotating pistons to deliver fluid, suitable for hydraulic systems. Radial piston pumps, with pistons arranged radially, provide high pressure and efficiency, making them ideal for heavy machinery and automotive applications.</p></p>
