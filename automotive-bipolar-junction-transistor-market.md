<p><ul><li>NPN Transistor</li><li>PNP Transistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Bipolar Junction Transistor (BJT) market comprises two primary types: NPN and PNP transistors. NPN transistors, which have three layers of semiconductor material with a negative-positive-negative configuration, are commonly used for switching and amplification in automotive applications. PNP transistors, with a positive-negative-positive configuration, are typically used in complementary circuits. Both types play essential roles in various automotive systems, including power management, signal processing, and electronic control units, ensuring efficient operation and reliable performance in modern vehicles.</p></p>
