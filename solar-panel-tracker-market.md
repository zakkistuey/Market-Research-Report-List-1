<p><p>The solar panel tracker market is divided into single-axis and dual-axis trackers, each optimizing solar energy capture. Single-axis trackers rotate on one axis, typically following the sun’s east-to-west movement, enhancing energy production by 25-35%. In contrast, dual-axis trackers move on two axes, adjusting to both the sun’s path and seasonal changes, increasing efficiency by up to 40% or more. Both technologies are crucial for maximizing solar output and improving the economic viability of solar power installations.</p></p>
