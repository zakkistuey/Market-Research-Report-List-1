<p><p>The residential microgrid market comprises three main types: AC microgrid systems, DC microgrid systems, and hybrid microgrid systems. AC microgrids integrate with existing electrical grids, allowing for flexibility in energy distribution and scalability. DC microgrids focus on renewable energy sources, optimizing energy efficiency for applications like solar and battery storage. Hybrid microgrid systems combine both AC and DC components, offering enhanced versatility and reliability by drawing from multiple energy sources. Each type caters to diverse energy needs and technologies in residential settings.</p></p>
