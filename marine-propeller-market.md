<p><strong>The Marine Propeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Controllable Pitch Propeller</li><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine propeller market comprises two primary types: controllable pitch propellers (CPP) and fixed pitch propellers (FPP). CPPs allow operators to adjust the blade angle while the propeller is in motion, providing enhanced maneuverability and efficiency in varying conditions, making them ideal for vessels with variable loads. In contrast, FPPs have a fixed blade angle, simplifying design and maintenance while offering reliable performance for specific speed conditions. Each type serves distinct applications based on operational requirements.</p></p>
