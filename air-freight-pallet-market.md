<p><ul><li>Lower Deck Pallet</li><li>Main Deck Pallet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air freight pallet market is categorized into two primary types: lower deck pallets and main deck pallets. Lower deck pallets are designed for use in the cargo hold of the aircraft, optimizing space and load distribution. They typically accommodate smaller shipments and are essential for maximizing lower capacity. In contrast, main deck pallets are used in the main cargo area and allow larger shipments, offering easier access for loading and unloading. Both types are crucial for efficient air cargo operations.</p></p>
