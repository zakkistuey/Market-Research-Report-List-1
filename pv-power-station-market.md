<p><p>The PV power station market consists of two primary types: on-grid and off-grid systems. On-grid PV power stations are connected to the main electricity grid, allowing excess power generation to be sold back and ensuring a stable energy supply, benefiting from net metering policies. In contrast, off-grid PV power stations operate independently, ideal for remote areas without grid access, providing energy autonomy. Both types cater to varying energy needs, environmental goals, and economic conditions, driving the growth of solar energy adoption.</p></p>
