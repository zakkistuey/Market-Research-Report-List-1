<p><p>The Low Noise Converter market is categorized into two main types: DC-DC converters and AC-DC converters. DC-DC converters are designed to convert direct current from one voltage level to another while minimizing electrical noise, making them ideal for sensitive electronic applications. In contrast, AC-DC converters convert alternating current to direct current, focusing on reducing noise to ensure stable operation in power-sensitive environments. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
