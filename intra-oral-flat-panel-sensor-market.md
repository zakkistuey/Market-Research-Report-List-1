<p><p>The intra-oral flat panel sensor market features two primary types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, making them ideal for detailed dental imaging. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer quicker readout times and lower power consumption, which can enhance patient comfort and workflow efficiency. Both technologies contribute to more accurate diagnostics and improved patient outcomes in dental practices worldwide.</p></p>
