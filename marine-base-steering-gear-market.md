<p><strong>The Marine Base Steering Gear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ram Type</li><li>Rotary Vane Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Base Steering Gear market consists of two main types: Ram Type and Rotary Vane Type. Ram Type steering gear employs hydraulic rams to control the rudder's position, offering precise maneuverability, especially in larger vessels. In contrast, Rotary Vane Type uses a rotating vane mechanism that allows for smoother and more efficient steering, making it suitable for smaller crafts and vessels needing quicker response times. Both types are essential for ensuring effective navigation and safety in marine operations.</p></p>
