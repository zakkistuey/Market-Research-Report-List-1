<p><p>The Connected Fire Detection Systems market comprises two primary types: Addressable and Conventional systems. Addressable Connected Fire Detection Systems feature advanced technology that allows each device to communicate its status individually, making it easier to identify the exact location of a fire or fault. In contrast, Conventional Connected Fire Detection Systems utilize simpler technology where multiple devices are grouped together, triggering alerts based on zone detection. Both systems enhance fire safety through connectivity, improving response times and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
