<p><p>The pH monitoring system market comprises several types, including glass type monitoring systems, ISFET monitoring systems, and others. Glass type systems utilize traditional glass electrodes to measure pH levels, offering high accuracy and reliability. ISFET (Ion-Sensitive Field Effect Transistor) monitoring systems provide a solid-state alternative, offering faster response times and increased durability in various environments. Other systems may include differential pH sensors and digital pH meters, catering to diverse applications across industries such as agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
