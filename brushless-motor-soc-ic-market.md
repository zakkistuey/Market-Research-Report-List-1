Control ICs manage the operational parameters of the motor, such as speed and direction, optimizing performance and efficiency. Drive ICs provide the necessary current and voltage to the motor coils, ensuring effective torque generation. Power ICs focus on energy management, delivering efficient power conversion and distribution. Together, these components enhance the functionality and efficiency of brushless motor systems across various applications.</p></p>
