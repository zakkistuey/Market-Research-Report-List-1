<p><p>The pump/tank fittings market encompasses various types, including IBC couplings, sweeps, flow meters, and dry disconnects. IBC couplings ensure secure connections for intermediate bulk containers, while sweeps facilitate smooth flow transitions in piping systems. Flow meters accurately measure the quantity of fluid transferred, crucial for inventory management and efficiency. Dry disconnects provide a safe, spill-free method for transferring liquids, enhancing safety during operations. Other fittings may include valves, hoses, and connectors, catering to diverse industrial fluid handling needs.</p></p>
